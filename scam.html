Spot the Scam

Real scam messages look convincing. Decide whether each one is a scam or genuine, and learn the tell-tale signs.

Message 1

🛡️

Nicely done

Score
  • Slow down. Urgency and threats are a scammer's favourite trick.
  • Check the sender and any web address. A name can be faked; the address is harder to fake.
  • Never share passwords or one-time codes, even if someone says they're from your bank.
  • Don't tap links in unexpected messages. Go to the app or website yourself.
  • When in doubt, check directly using a number or website you find yourself.

How to check anything

  • Were you expecting this message?
  • Does the sender or web address look right?
  • Is it rushing you or making threats?
  • Is it asking for money, passwords or codes?
  • Can you check with the company directly instead?
